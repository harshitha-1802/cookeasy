About Recipe Finder

Your go-to solution for finding delicious recipes with the ingredients you have!

Who We Are

Recipe Finder is an AI-powered web application that helps users find recipes based on the ingredients they have. No more food waste—just creative, easy-to-make dishes at your fingertips!

Our Mission

We aim to make cooking easy, fun, and accessible for everyone by providing personalized recipes instantly.

How It Works

  1. Enter the ingredients you have.
  2. Get AI-powered recipe recommendations.
  3. Filter by vegetarian or non-vegetarian options.
  4. Follow easy step-by-step instructions.
